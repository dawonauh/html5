How to make Pepperoni Pizza

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Pepperoni
  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce

Steps:

  1. Mix flour with water
  2. Knead the dough and use a roller pin to flatten the dough
  3. Spread tomato sauce on the dough
  4. Spread Cheese on the dough
  5. Add pepperoni on the dough
  6. Bake for 20 mins at 400 F
  7. After 20 mins take out pizza, leave to cool for 5 mins and enjoy
